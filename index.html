Tip of the week
Feeling stuck with your Instagram strategy? Consider the 4:3:2 method from Social Media Examiner to tell a story every nine squares. Try:
  • Four posts that add value and help ypur ideal client
  • Three posts a that create connections with your ideal client
  • Two posts that promote or sell your services
Yes, sales are important, but there's more to establishing close bonds with your audience. Make sure you're dedicating a good chunk of time to getting to know and building a solid relationship with the people who follow you. This way, when you're ready to sell, you'll have an engaged audience ready and waiting to buy your product.
